Privacy Policy

Effective January 1, 2025

SwipeSweep doesn't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data or photos. Everything stays on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. No accounts, no analytics, no tracking.

Photo library access

SwipeSweep accesses your Photos library only to:

  • Display photos for your review
  • Move discarded photos to Recently Deleted

No photos are uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

Data storage

All preferences are stored locally using Apple's standard frameworks. We run no servers.

Deleting SwipeSweep removes all app data. Your photos stay in Apple Photos unaffected.

Third-party services

None. No analytics, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs.

Children's privacy

We collect no data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date above and notify you in-app.

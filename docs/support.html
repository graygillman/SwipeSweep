Does SwipeSweep permanently delete my photos?

No. Swiping left moves photos to your iPhone's Recently Deleted album, which keeps them for 30 days. You can recover any photo from the Photos app during that window.

Why does SwipeSweep need photo access?

To display your photos and move the ones you discard to Recently Deleted. Photos are never uploaded or transmitted — all processing is on your device.

I denied photo access. How do I fix it?

Open Settings → SwipeSweep → Photos → Full Access. Return to the app and it'll load automatically.

Can I undo after moving to the next photo?

Yes. Tap the undo button at any time to reverse your most recent decision. You can undo multiple times in a row.

Photos aren't loading. What should I do?

Confirm photo permissions are set to Full Access in Settings. If it persists, try closing and reopening the app. Still stuck? Reach out below.

Does SwipeSweep collect any data?

Zero. No accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. See our Privacy Policy for details.
